Overview

King StarBoard® ST is a unique and advanced high-density polyethylene building sheet that lasts a lifetime. It is the product of a proprietary process called K-Stran™, the most advanced manufacturing process for superior flatness and consistency. This polymer sheet is 25 percent stiffer than the original King StarBoard®.

Advantages

  • matte gloss finish on both sides of sheets
  • excellent scratch resistance, making it ideal for cabinetry and furniture
  • 25 percent stiffer than original King StarBoard®
  • environmentally stabilized to withstand the harshest outdoor conditions
  • will not rust, delaminate, or rot when exposed to UV, humidity, or water
  • versatile, durable, and colorfast, and never needs painting or refinishing
  • works like wood and is easy to fabricate using common woodworking tools and techniques

Applications

  • Concession Stands
  • Dugout Areas in Sports Arenas
  • Equipment
  • Furniture
  • Healthcare Case Goods
  • Indoor and Outdoor Cabinets
  • Kick Plates
  • Lockers
  • Outdoor Kitchens
  • Playgrounds
  • Pool Areas
  • Tabletops and Counters
